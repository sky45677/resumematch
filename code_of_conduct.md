# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors to ResumeMatch AI pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, location, disability, ethnicity, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, gender identity, or political views.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be helpful and patient with newcomers.
- Focus on the code, not the person.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or exclusionary behavior will be handled by the project maintainer and may result in a permanent ban from the repository.
